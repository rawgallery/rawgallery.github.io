Welcome to RAW__GALLERY.

This is my homepage for a starter project I'm doing within my visual/graphic design studio, 'RAW__GALLERY'. My goal is to quickly learn visual design techniques via HTML and CSS. Cue applause. For some content, here is a bit about me: I like to describe myself as a "matchmaker" between users and products, driven by my passion for leveraging the intrinsic power of brands in new ways. Combining a background in logistics and supply chain with in-field marketing and UX experience, I utilize qualitative research methods and highlight product characteristics and demographic themes in order to cultivate a relationship between a user and product or service. In my experience as a brand manager and marketer, I've facilitated digital promotions and curated digital content for promotional campaigns featuring Rick Ross, Jennifer Lopez, and Donald Glover, and more. I've contributed to several large-scale UX strategy projects, integrating comprehensive visual design, closely examining cultural themes and trends, and experimenting with disruptive consumer-facing technology- most recently including blockchain in logistics contract management services. I thoroughly enjoy and am comfortable diving into existing processes, and establishing a clear distinction between current- and target-state. While working towards my Bachelor's degree in Logistics, Materials & Supply Chain Management, I completed several business analysis internships at Fiat-Chrysler Automobiles N.V., and later for Cisco Systems, in global fulfillment and operations planning, respectively.
